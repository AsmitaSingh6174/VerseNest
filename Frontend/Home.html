Whispers of Dawn

In the quiet moments before sunrise,
When the world holds its breath in anticipation,
I find the words that have been hiding,
Behind the veil of night's contemplation.

Whispers of dawn break through the silence,
Painting the sky with promises anew,
Each ray of light, a verse of hope,
Each shadow, a memory of you.

Midnight Thoughts

Stars punctuate the darkness,
Like periods at the end of heaven's sentences.
I collect them in jars of memory,
To illuminate my darkest thoughts.

In this midnight hour,
Words become constellations,
Mapping the universe within.

Echoes of Time

Time-worn pages whisper tales of old,
Ink-stained fingers trace the words of gold.
In dusty tomes and leather-bound dreams,
The past and present intertwine, it seems.

Like autumn leaves that fall with grace,
Our stories drift through time and space.
Yet in these verses, we remain,
Immortal souls that words contain.

Spilled Ink

Words spill across the page,
Like ink from an overturned bottle.
Some form perfect lines,
Others create beautiful accidents.

I am the vessel,
The container of thoughts,
But sometimes the best poetry,
Comes when I'm broken open.
